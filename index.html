/ Technical Framework

SPORA System Architecture

🧠

Cyber-Physical System

5-Level Hierarchical Restoration Intelligence — a layered decision architecture enabling real-time adaptive responses across the full operational environment.

5Mission Planning & Strategic Control
4Autonomous Task Scheduling
3Sensor Fusion & Environmental Perception
2Actuation & Mobility Control
1Physical Hardware & Field Interaction
💧

Water Management

Integrated Atmospheric Water Extraction (AWE) paired with moisture-retaining hydrogel capsules embedded at seed deployment sites — enabling off-grid irrigation in hyper-arid environments with zero external water dependency.

🛰️

Navigation & Mobility

Solar-powered autonomous mobility guided by multi-modal sensor fusion: LiDAR, GPS-RTK, thermal imaging, and soil moisture arrays. Combines A* graph search with RRT probabilistic planning for terrain-adaptive pathfinding across dynamic desert landscapes.
